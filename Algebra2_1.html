1.1: Real Numbers and Number Operations

Subsets of the Real Numbers

Whole Numebers

\(0,1,2,3,4,...\)

Integers

\(...,-3,-2,-1,0,1,2,3,...\)

Rational Numbers

Such as \(\frac{3}{4},\frac{1}{3},\) and \(\frac{-4}{1}\) (or \(-4\)). When written as decimals, rational numbers terminate or repeat.

Irrational Numbers

Real numbers that are not rational, such as \(\sqrt{2}\) and \(\pi\). When written as decimals, irrational numbers neither terminate nor repeat.
